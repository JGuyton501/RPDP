About us

RAplus+ is a web application to manage programs, archive programs, garner peer/resident feedback, and facilitate staff communication (RCD, AD, etc.). The residential living area at Washington University is divided between multiple Residential Colleges and this application will manage what events, along with floor and res college budgets, are going on on every floor of every rescollege.
